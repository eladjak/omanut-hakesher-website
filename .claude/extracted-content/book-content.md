## Book Structure (4 Parts, 10 Chapters)

### Part 1: מבפנים החוצה (From the Inside Out)
> "אי אפשר למצוא את האדם הנכון אם אתה לא מכיר את עצמך"

**Chapter 1: הסיפורים שאנחנו מספרים לעצמנו (The Stories We Tell Ourselves)**
- Based on weeks 1-2 of the course
- Topics: Limiting beliefs, growth through difficulty, personal responsibility, automatic thought patterns
- Key concept: "Rowan Principle" - stop making excuses, start acting
- Exercises: Rewrite 3 self-stories; gratitude list of 5 people
- Story: Noam, 34, software engineer who believed "all the good ones are taken"
- ~3,500 words

**Chapter 2: שפות האהבה - מעבר לתיאוריה (Love Languages - Beyond Theory)**
- Based on week 3 of the course
- Topics: 5 love languages in depth, identifying your own language, practical application
- ~7,200 words

**Chapter 3: תקשורת שמחברת (Communication That Connects)**
- Based on week 5 of the course
- Topics: Emotional vocabulary, needs-based communication, reciprocity
- ~6,800 words

### Part 2: העומק (The Depth)
> "תקשורת טובה היא לא לדעת מה להגיד. זה לדעת איך להקשיב"

**Chapter 4: ניהול קונפליקטים בזוגיות (Managing Conflicts in Relationships)**
- Topics: Healthy conflict vs destructive fighting, repair attempts, Gottman research
- ~6,000 words

**Chapter 5: אינטימיות ופגיעות (Intimacy and Vulnerability)**
- Topics: Paradox of intimacy, 4 layers of intimacy, vulnerability scale, 36 Questions
- Story: Eden who froze when asked personal questions; Yonatan who fled every relationship after month 2
- ~7,200 words

**Chapter 6: גבולות בריאים (Healthy Boundaries)**
- Topics: 5 types of boundaries (physical, emotional, time, digital, values), setting boundaries vs control
- Story: Ortal who gave everything and was left empty; Nir who felt suffocated
- ~5,970 words

**Chapter 7: סליחה ופיוס (Forgiveness and Reconciliation)**
- Topics: What forgiveness is/isn't, 7-step process, self-forgiveness, Israeli cultural context
- Story: Yotam stuck 3 years after betrayal; Anat & Roni rebuilding trust
- ~5,550 words

### Part 3: המפגש (The Encounter)
> "אי אפשר למצוא אהבה מהספה. צריך לצאת לדרך"

**Chapter 8: אומנות הדייט (The Art of Dating)**
- Topics: 10 golden rules for first dates, phone calls as bridge, digital dating challenges, post-date protocols
- Story: Shira who came to dates with question lists; Amir who was "perfect" but not authentic
- ~7,500 words

**Chapter 9: כימיה - מה זה באמת (Chemistry - What It Really Is)**
- Topics: Dopamine/noradrenaline/oxytocin/serotonin, 3 brain systems (Dr. Helen Fisher), 4 chemistry myths, active listening as chemistry builder
- Story: Ori & Liron - no immediate chemistry, which is exactly why it worked; Tal who confused anxiety for "sparks"
- ~6,600 words

**Chapter 10: מחויבות והחלטה (Commitment and Decision)**
- Topics: 5 commitment fears, commitment as decision not emotion, 20 questions before deciding, 5 pillars (respect, trust, communication, growth, enjoyment), Gottman 5:1 ratio
- Story: Tamar & Itai - "I love you but can't commit"; Noa who fled every good relationship
- ~6,100 words

### Part 4: Closing
**סיום: חוקי הזוגיות (Closing: The Rules of Relationships)**
- Principles for long-term relationships
- How to start a formal relationship
- The letter to yourself
- ~2,500 words

### Appendices

## Key Concepts & Exercises for Website Use

### Core Concepts:
1. **"הדרך" (The Way)** - A structured journey, not random tips
2. **"פנוי לקשר" (Available for Connection)** - The mental/emotional state needed
3. **Limiting stories vs empowering stories** - Rewriting self-narratives
4. **14 common fears** on the path to relationship
5. **5 love languages** - practical application beyond theory
6. **Active listening** as chemistry builder
7. **Chemistry vs compatibility** - what really works long-term
8. **Commitment as daily choice** - "I choose you, today, again"
9. **Gottman's 5:1 ratio** - 5 positive interactions per 1 negative
10. **4 layers of intimacy** - social, personal, emotional, absolute

### Selected Exercises (for free content/lead magnets):
1. "Write 3 stories you tell yourself about relationships - then rewrite them"
2. "14 fears - mark your top 3 and write what's the worst that could happen"
3. "Emotion journal: 1 week of 'what am I feeling now?' 3x daily"
4. "6 courage exercises: from small (smile at a stranger) to big (invite someone for coffee)"
5. "Date report template"
6. "10 great questions for dates"
7. "Listening exercise: 10-minute conversation without talking about yourself"
8. "Fear map: identify, understand, plan"

## Writing Voice Guidelines
- **Who speaks**: Elad - warm facilitator, authentic, with humor and sensitivity
- **Address**: Direct to reader singular ("you")
- **Tone**: Like a deep conversation with a good friend who also understands the subject
- **Language**: Accessible Hebrew, no academic jargon. Simple words for deep ideas
- **Structure per chapter**: Opening story > Problem > Insight > Practical tools > Field story > Exercise > Sharp summary
- **Rules**: Short sentences, short paragraphs, lots of white space. Break the "fourth wall" sometimes.
